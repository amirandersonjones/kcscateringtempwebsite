Meet Our Culinary Artist


As a young girl, I manifested a desire to cook as I was helping my mother in the kitchen.She was such an inspiration and with her we developed this passion for creative cooking that actually tasted good! The more time we spent in the kitchen the more my creative passion for food increased. I started becoming very proficient in planning menus for family gatherings and holidays; developing recipes; and later supervising the preparation of food with my own children! In that sense, you can say that this has become a family tradition of preparing and cooking food that nourishes not only people's stomachs, but also their spirit of family. I hope to share that fulfilling experience with you!
